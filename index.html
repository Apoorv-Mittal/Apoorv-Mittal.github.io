Who is Apoorv?



I am an undergraduate from the University of Maryland-College Park.
I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer science and expect to graduate in the year 2020.
But really I am still a kid in front of my parents.
My sister says that I am crazy.
I have schoolmates who say I am a very straight forward person.
There are strangers who stereotype me as a very serious person and are surprised once they get to know me.

But to tell you the truth, I don't know much about me.
As far as I know, I am a simple, kind, fun loving guy who can be crazy sometimes.
I like to stay busy.
I prefer to do things my way.
I work hard for what I have passion for.
I can excel if I am given the opportunity.
I am not sure if I have achieved something in life or not but one thing is true-I will become better compared to what I was yesterday and keep on improving myself day by day!

And this is who I am.
